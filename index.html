What is RandCRM?

RandCRM is a very special tool for customer Resource Management. The features inside of it are what make it special:
  • Free Unlimited Data Storage, customer contact, audio, video, and text notes.
  • Use Machine learning to predict likelihood of sale.
  • Sort your customers by likelihood to buy or highest expected purchase.
  • Connect your data to mailchimp or other automated messaging.
